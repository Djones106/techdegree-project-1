What I'm Reading

The Lady Sherlock Series | Sherry Thomas

Writen by USA Today bestselling author Sherry Thomas brings a refreshing take on Sherlock Holmes. The characters are memorable and dynamic. The mysteries that Holmes solves are intriguing and keeps you guessing throughout each book.

What I'm Watching

Castlevania | Netflix Series

Created by Warren Ellis. Inspired by a classic video game, this animated series follows a vampire hunter and comrades who fight otherwordly beasts. This series has 4 seasons and is not for the weak of heart.

What's on Repeat

Make it Right | BTS - Map Of The Soul: 7

BTS is a South Korean boy band with seven members. Their debut was in 2013. One of my favorite songs was released in February 2020, their fourth consecutive album to debut as the number one album on the Billboard 200 chart.
